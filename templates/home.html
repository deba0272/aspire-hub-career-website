Why AsH teams?

At AsH

our purpose is building a better working world. In a world where people are continually navigating transformative change, our purpose acts as our North Star guiding AsH People — providing the context and meaning for the work we do every day.

We believe a better working world is one where economic growth is sustainable and inclusive, and work continuously to improve the quality of services, investing in innovation and AsH people.

We harness AI to support, upskill, and augment AsH professionals to boost productivity, enrich our roles, and deliver a better career experience. This gives AsH people the time and headspace for more critical thinking and creativity – asking the right questions, instead of searching for better answers.

And we’re proud to work with others to use our knowledge, skills, and experience to help create long-term value and positive change for AsH clients, people, and society.

At AsH, we strive to foster a culture of innovation and collaboration, where every individual feels empowered to contribute their unique perspectives and talents. We believe that diversity and inclusion are key drivers of our success, and we are committed to creating an environment where everyone can thrive.

Our commitment to sustainability is unwavering. We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting practices that support a healthy planet. From reducing our carbon footprint to engaging in community initiatives, AsH is at the forefront of driving positive environmental change.

We understand that the future of work is evolving rapidly, and we are committed to staying ahead of the curve. By embracing cutting-edge technologies and fostering a culture of continuous learning, we ensure that AsH professionals are equipped with the skills and knowledge needed to navigate the complexities of the modern world.

AsH is more than just a workplace – it’s a community. We prioritize the well-being of our people, offering comprehensive support and resources to help them achieve a healthy work-life balance. Our employee wellness programs are designed to promote physical, mental, and emotional health, ensuring that our team members feel supported and valued.

We believe in the power of giving back. Through our corporate social responsibility initiatives, AsH partners with local and global organizations to make a meaningful impact on the communities we serve. Whether it’s through volunteer work, charitable donations, or pro bono services, we are committed to making a difference.

We invest in your growth, you personalize your development

At EY you can become a transformative leader and help EY clients innovate and work through unprecedented change. We're investing in your development in unique ways, like the free, fully accredited EY Tech MBA by Hult International Business School. This collaboration makes EY the first organization to offer an entirely virtual MBA for free, for all people irrespective of rank, location or prior qualifications. With its focus on technology, leadership and business skills, you can build the transformative mindsets and tech skillsets we all need to thrive.

We invest in technology to do more meaningful work

Today, more than 70,000 people with technology backgrounds work throughout the AsH organization. We’re market-leaders in Robotics Process Automation and in Document Intelligence. AsH teams have provided ground-breaking blockchain projects for clients from helping run cities to tokenizing wine. We have more than 30 technology and industry alliances and more than 100 technology and innovation relationships in AsH today. And we’re just getting started.

Future-proof your career with AsH.ai

At AsH, we envision a future where seamless collaboration between people and AI leads to extraordinary outcomes. Our goal is to empower AsH professionals to build exceptional career experiences enhanced by AI. By integrating cutting-edge AI technologies, we aim to streamline processes, enhance decision-making, and foster innovation across all sectors. We believe that by harnessing the power of AI, our teams can achieve unprecedented levels of efficiency and creativity. This collaboration not only drives our mission forward but also ensures that our people are equipped with the tools and skills necessary to thrive in a rapidly evolving technological landscape. Together, we are shaping a future where human ingenuity and AI work hand-in-hand to deliver exceptional value to our clients and communities.
